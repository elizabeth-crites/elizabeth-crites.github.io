Elizabeth C. Crites

My current research interests include the following:
  • mathematical security of zero-knowledge proof systems
  • signature schemes (including threshold/multi-signatures and blind signatures)
  • distributed key generation protocols
  • randomness beacons and VRFs
  • decentralized identity systems
  • simple and efficient anonymous credential systems
  • privacy-preserving cryptographic primitives
